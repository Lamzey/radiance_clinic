About Us

Welcome to Radiance Health and Wellness Clinic

Led by Melodie Mbebi, FNP-C, MSN, BSN, RN, we are committed to delivering high-quality, patient-centered care to individuals and families in our community. With over a decade of experience in primary care and a passion for promoting true health and wellness, we strive to create a warm and welcoming environment where you feel heard, valued, and empowered.

Our dedicated team addresses the unique needs of each patient, building strong relationships to foster trust and advocate for your overall well-being.
